Stop making apps and start making PWA's

A technical discussion in favour of switching over to the Progressive Web Apps train

We've all been through this: downloading a several-megabyte-sized native app that does little more than just display a Web page or, even worse, the most mundane and ordinary table with plain boring text. Even though the developers made it using the latest and greatest mobile framework that has all the bells and whistles, after many months of negotiating with Management, one can't help but wonder: why going through the trials and tribulations of making an app that displays a Web page, and not making a simple yet well-designed Web page?
