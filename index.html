Keeping design simple

This site was conceived using a minimalist aesthetic approach to its layout and functionalities in order to improve, among other things, its readability, ease of parsing and loading speed on lower bandwidth connections or when using an unstable Internet service. Enjoy the experience of fast access to pages and reading without any distractions.
